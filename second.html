The rules for playing Memory

In every job that must be done, there is an element of fun. You find the fun, and - SNAP - the job's a game! - Mary Poppins"

The Objective of the Game

You win the game once all of the cards have been matched!

The Game Play

  • In the begining of the game, the cards are shuffled.
  • Turn over any two cards.
  • If the two cards match, then leave it. You got a pair!
  • If they don't match, turn them back over.
  • Watch and remember what images were on the cards.
  • The game is over when all the cards have been matched.
